Research

Peer-reviewed work published to IEEE/IJCT standards, 2025.

📄 Peer-Reviewed Publication
IJCT / IEEE Standard · 2025 · Co-authored
NUTS: Nutritional Understanding & Tracking System — A Mobile-First Approach to Automated Dietary Monitoring

Co-led the design, engineering, and evaluation of a cross-platform mobile nutrition tracker using React Native and Firebase. The system employs ML Kit's on-device food recognition to automate nutrient extraction from photographs, significantly reducing manual data entry burden. The paper documents the full system architecture, model performance benchmarks, and a structured user study validating dietary tracking accuracy against established nutritional databases.
